Island Dining

Restaurants and grocery options on the island

Restaurants

The island currently has 10 restaurants serving a variety of cuisines.

Local Fish and Rice (5 Restaurants)

Five restaurants serve mostly local fish and rice dishes.

American-Style Meals (3 Restaurants)

Three restaurants serve American-style meals.

Pan-Asian Cuisine (2 Restaurants)

Two restaurants serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Supermarkets

The island has two supermarkets.

Smaller Grocery Stores

The island has two smaller grocery stores.

24-Hour Convenience Store

One convenience store is open 24 hours a day.

⚠️ Alcohol Service Hours

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM.

The drinking age on the island is 18 years old.
