About the Island

Welcome to your tropical paradise in the Pacific! This small island offers an incredible variety of experiences in less than 500 square miles.

What makes us special: From our active volcano and lush rainforests to pristine beaches and vibrant local culture, we offer authentic island experiences you won't find anywhere else. Our indigenous population of 20,000 welcomes visitors with genuine warmth and hospitality.

Terrain & Geography: Sandy and rocky beaches, safe harbor, tropical rainforests, mountainous interior with active volcano

Main Areas: Island City (historic center), Yellow Leaf Bay (beaches), Merriton Landing (entertainment & dining hub)
