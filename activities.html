Things to Do

Activities and attractions on the island

Popular Activities

Most people visit the island to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. However, there are many other things to do.

Beaches

White, sandy beaches encircle Yellow Leaf Bay near Island City. The island also has rocky beaches.

Rainforest

  • Hikes in the rainforest
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest

Volcano

Visit the island's active volcano.

Water Activities

  • Snorkeling
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Boat tours of the island

Sightseeing Tours

  • Bus tours of the island
  • Boat tours of the island
  • Helicopter rides

Culture & Entertainment

  • Local history museum
  • Art galleries
  • Several pubs, including a microbrewery
  • A new dance club
  • Movie theater

Recreation

  • Arcade
  • Bowling
  • Nine-hole golf course (should be operational by next year)

📍 Merriton Landing

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

⚠️ National Holidays

The island enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.
